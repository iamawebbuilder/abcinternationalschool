What we do

We provide a supportive and inclusive environment that promotes both academic excellence and personal growth. It engages students with a diverse curriculum, encourages critical thinking, and cultivates strong relationships between teachers, students, and the community..

Extracurricular Activities

We are very keen on every child participating in extracurricular activities because we believe it enhances students' social skills, creativity, and overall well-being, allowing them to explore interests beyond academics. These activities also promote teamwork, discipline, and a sense of belonging, ultimately enriching the educational experience.

Secured and Serene Environment

We operate a secure and serene primary and secondary school environment where safety and mental well-being are prioritised,thus fostering an atmosphere where pupils and students feel protected and valued. This supportive setting encourages open communication, respect among peers, and a focus on academic and personal growth, enabling students to thrive.

We encourage creativity

ABC International School encourages creativity by integrating arts and innovative projects into the curriculum, allowing students to explore their interests.We also provide collaborative spaces and resources that inspire imaginative thinking, enabling learners to express themselves through various mediums and approaches.

An Elarning School

Our elearning portal affords our pupils and students the opportunity to a dynamic virtual learning environment designed to engage and inspire them. With interactive lessons, personalized support, and a diverse curriculum, we empower learners to thrive academically and socially, both within the school premises and as well as from the comfort of their homes. All our Continuous Assessment Tests and Exams are also written using our VLE.
